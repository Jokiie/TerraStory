------ English -----------------------------------------------

Hi ! I made a mod about Maplestory, because Terraria and Maplestory are my 2 favorite games and
with which I play the most. I have never code before, and I learned everything on my own, and in english(it's not my
native language at all)! So I did a bit of everything with what little knowledge and expertise I have. 
Expect a lot of bugs, but don't worry, I am correcting as I go. Here is an overview of what I have accomplished:

- 82 new NPCs
- 1 new biome (Ludibrium, Lego biome)
- 284 new items (Materials, weapons, armor, ammunition and more!)
- 55 new blocks and decorations, the majority concerning Ludibrium
- 36 buffs and debuffs
- 5 new mounts, including 1 a little buggy, I think xD
- 5 bosses, But 1 or 2 were tests, they need a little love ...
- Several vanilla armor have been modified and are subject to many other changes
- UI completely changed to match the one in MapleStory
- Experience, level and stats system
- 2 new class: the cannoneer and the thief
- Content up to Wall of fresh, for the moment

I would of course prefer to have all my content in large pixels as terraria at the base. But as
I don't have a lot of spriting experience and thought I would make this mod for fun,
I used the Maplestory sprites a lot. At the moment I am gradually working to rework
my mod so that it matches well with Terraria.

Thanks to the Luggage pet mod, with the open source code, I could make possible the 
looting pet from Maplestory, with some changes & to the Tmodloader discord for answering some questions ! :)

Jokie.

 -Fixed rain music.
